About

Able is an app that uses Google Maps API software ot help users locate public places that are and aren't handicap accessible. There is information about wheelchair and parking accessibility, accommodations for the deaf and blind such as braille, audio descriptions sign language, closed captions, and accessibility for those in the autism spectrum who might require a quieter space. This information would be provided both by establishments and by the appâ€™s users. Unlike other apps, it will contain a wealth of information for people with disabilities of all sorts, and users will be able to make comments about their experience in certain places.
